BASEBALL

Baseball is a game where its main goal is score more than the opponent team, by doing this, you hit the ball hard and far enough for you to take enough time to reach a base,
depending on how confident you are, the game is about confidence and predictibility as a batter whilst the catcher is more on how quick you can go to catch and return back
to prevent the hitters from going home. if you are confident enough as a batter and reach through all the bases and back to where you started, you'll have a home run which
you can score quicker.

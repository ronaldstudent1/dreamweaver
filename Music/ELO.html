ELECTRIC LIGHT ORCHESTRA

Electric Light Orchestra is a band formed in the 70's by Jeff Lyne, Roy Wood and Bev Bevan.The main goal is to create a mixture of modern rock and pop music to blend in
with classical arrangements. the bands name is a pivoted between two styles that was popular in the 40s-60s. During the 70s-80s, ELO had relased top 10's albums and singles
which one of them became a succesful hit being "Out Of The Blue" in 1977.unfortunately ELO was disbanded in 1986 when the lead singer, Jeff Lyne lost interest in the group
and forced-disband it. because of this, Bev Bevam decided to form his own band being ELO Part II which had some other members of the old ELO members, this lasted until
1999 when ELO went inactive until 2014.

during ELO's span, the band had made around 50+ million copies worldwide, they've achieved about 19 CRIA, 21 RIAA and 38 BPI awards, they made up the 27 top 40
songs in the UK chart. and 15 top 20 in the US. The band also achieve and holds the record of having the most billboard hot 100 top 40 hits of all time. in 2017 the 3 original
members who founded the band including a decease key member of the group Richard Tandy, was awarded and inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of FAme
